Hardik Monga

Digital Identity

An interactive installation exploring the concept of self in the digital age.

Year

2021

Categories

Interactive Installation

Digital Identity is an interactive installation that examines how our sense of self is shaped by digital technologies and online platforms. The work invites participants to engage with a series of interactive elements that reflect and distort their physical presence, creating a dialogue between the physical and virtual self.

The installation uses a combination of cameras, sensors, and projection mapping to create responsive environments that react to the movements and actions of participants. As visitors move through the space, they encounter different interpretations of their digital presence, from data visualizations to algorithmic representations.

This project explores questions of privacy, surveillance, and self-representation in the digital age. By making visible the processes through which our identities are captured, analyzed, and reproduced in digital spaces, Digital Identity encourages critical reflection on our relationship with technology and the ways in which it shapes our understanding of ourselves and others.
