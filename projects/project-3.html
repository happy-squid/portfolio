Hardik Monga

Chromatic Reverie

Abstract expressions through color and texture.

Year

2022

Categories

Painting Abstract

Chromatic Reverie explores the emotional impact of color and texture in abstract composition. This series of paintings investigates how different color combinations and textural elements can evoke specific moods and sensations in the viewer.

Each piece in this collection represents a different emotional state, from tranquility and joy to tension and melancholy. Through layering techniques and experimental approaches to material application, the works create a rich visual experience that invites prolonged engagement.

The creative process for this series involved extensive color studies and material experimentation. By embracing both chance and intention, these compositions balance spontaneity with careful consideration of formal elements, resulting in works that feel both organic and structured.
